    About Us

    E-Registry.com is a blockchain based platform where seller and buyer interact with each other for transferring & registering their property documents digitally.

    • To provide a transparent and efficient way of property registration
    • Reduce multiple paper work by making the process digital
    • No one can forge the documents as blockchain provides immutability

    We are making a system to conveniently transfer property ownership from one person to another person digitally. By using blockchain technology in our system, we are making our system immutable.Deploy the complete registry data on the blockchain network.
